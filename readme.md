# Raven Logistics UK - Community Blacklist

A comprehensive blacklist system for Raven Logistics UK community members who have violated community standards and policies.

## About

This repository maintains a record of users who have been blacklisted from the Raven Logistics UK community on various platforms. The blacklist includes users who have engaged in misconduct, security risks, harassment, or violations of community standards.

## Data Structure

The blacklist is stored in `blacklist.json` with the following fields:

- **discord_id**: The user's Discord account ID (if applicable)
- **discord_username**: The user's Discord username (if applicable)
- **reason**: The specific reason for blacklisting
- **moderator**: The team or individual who made the decision

## Usage

This blacklist file can be integrated with moderation tools and bots to automatically enforce community standards across various platforms.

## Contributing

Blacklist additions should only be made by authorized Raven Logistics UK moderators and ownership team members through the appropriate review and approval process.
